Omelette Recipe

This is a page for a quick and easy Baklava recipe

Hopefully this omlette is good, we shall see.

Ingredients:

Servings: 1 Person

  • 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • [Optional] 1 cup chopped portobello mushrooms
  • [Optional] 1 Tbsp chopped fresh onions
  • [Optional] 1 cup Kale - stems removed
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 cup freshly shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

Follow these instructions in order

  1. In a nonstick pan, add the olive oil, chopped mushrooms [if using], and fresh onion [if using]. Saute for 7-8 minutes on medium heat. Remove from pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan add the kale leaves [if using]. Saute for 10 minutes on medium-low heat. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, crack the eggs and whisk well.
  4. Melt the butter in the pan over medium heat.
  5. Add the beaten eggs and let the omlette cook for about 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  6. While the omlette is cooking add in butter and cheese and lightly stir the middle of the omelette, keeping the inside from hardening.
  7. Add your cheese and continue to lightly stir - again keeping the inside from hardeing.
  8. Add your other veggies at this time if you want.
  9. Holding your pan securely upright, let the top of the omelette fold itself down over the other side and let cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  10. Serve while hot.
